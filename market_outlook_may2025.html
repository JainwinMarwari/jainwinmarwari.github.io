Market Outlook: May 2025

Macro Trends Impacting Small Caps

May 2025 brings a unique macroeconomic environment where small caps are gaining momentum. With inflation moderating and interest rate cuts on the horizon, capital is flowing back into growth-centric small cap names. Domestic consumption remains strong, aided by rural recovery and lower fuel prices.

At Jainwin Marwari, we’re focused on identifying fundamentally strong businesses that will benefit most from these cyclical tailwinds. In this piece, we’ll walk through a few sectors that excite us right now and share how we’re positioning for the next few quarters.

Favorable Sectors

  • Capital Goods: Strong order books driven by private capex.
  • Consumer Discretionary: Rural consumption rebound.
  • Financial Services: Well-capitalized NBFCs gaining share in Tier 2/3 cities.

We continue to believe in bottom-up stock picking while keeping macro as a guide—not a driver. The goal is steady compounding, not chasing momentum.
